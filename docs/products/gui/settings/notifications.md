# Notifications

This section details how to configure push notifications, sound alerts, and toast messages for application events.

![Notifications Settings](/img/products/gui/settings/notifications-settings.png)

## Telegram Bots

Integrate the trading bot with Telegram to receive real-time execution alerts, daily summaries, and control the bot remotely.

- **Add Bot**: Configure a new Telegram bot by providing its alias and your `Chat ID`.
- You can add multiple bots, and selectively enable or disable them.
- Use the **Edit** or **Remove** buttons to manage your existing bots.

## Microsoft Teams

Send structured alert cards to a Microsoft Teams channel.

- **Enable Teams**: Toggle Teams notifications on or off.
- **Webhook URL**: Paste the incoming Webhook URL generated from your Teams channel connector.
- **Test Connection**: Sends a test payload to the configured webhook to verify connectivity.

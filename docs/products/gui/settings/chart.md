# Chart

This section explains how to configure chart data limits, drawing styles, and visualization parameters.

![Chart Settings](/img/products/gui/settings/chart-settings.png)

## Display Options

- **Show Volume**: Toggles the visibility of the volume histogram at the bottom of the candlestick chart.

## Candle Colors

Personalize the visual representation of price action on the chart.

- **Color Presets**: Quickly apply predefined color schemes such as `Traditional`, `Solid Classic`, `Monochrome`, `MetaTrader 5`, or `Vivid`.
- **Custom Colors**: If `Custom` is selected, you can explicitly define:
  - **Up Candle Color** / **Up Body Color**: Colors for bullish candlesticks.
  - **Down Candle Color** / **Down Body Color**: Colors for bearish candlesticks.

# Market Watch

Market Watch is the primary entry point for managing symbols and switching the active trading instrument.

## Adding Symbols

1. Click the "Add Symbol" button or press Ctrl+N.
2. Select a symbol from the pop-up list dialog.
3. Confirm to append the instrument to your watch list.

## Common Operations

- Keep your watch list focused on symbols relevant to the current session.
- Click a symbol in the list to instantly update the active chart context.
- Use in combination with the timeframe selector for multi-timeframe analysis.
